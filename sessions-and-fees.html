Sessions & Fees

Initial Consultation

I offer an initial consultation which lasts between 30 - 40 minutes. This is an information gathering session and enables us to discuss why you are seeking help and how hypnotherapy can help you by explaining how the brain works so that you leave with a full understanding of how it relates to your particular issue. You will also be given a Relaxation CD which you will be required to listen to as part of your commitment to treatment.

Subsequent Hypnotherapy Sessions

Subsequent sessions last for approximately 50 minutes - 1 hour. The amount of sessions can vary depending on your individual needs.

During the first part of each session we'll discuss the progress you have made, encouraging you to express yourself in a positive way, focusing on solutions rather than dwelling on problems.

I will help you to imagine your preferred future, a future where you have a more useful perspective on the quality of your life. I'll then guide you into a relaxed state of hypnosis so that we can consolidate what we have discussed.

Smoking Cessation

Smoking cessation is a one off session lasting approximately 90 minutes - 2 hours and includes CD support. No initial consultation is required. Willpower not included!
