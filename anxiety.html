Anxiety

Anxiety can be described as a general feeling of unease and everyone has experienced it at some point in their life from feeling a little tense about starting a new job or worrying about an exam to feeling fearful about public speaking.

Some forms of anxiety, if short lived, can be useful, for instance when taking an exam you may feel more alert and your performance enhanced.

However, when high levels of anxiety are sustained for long periods of time you can start to feel overwhelmed, feel unable to cope which can affect all areas of your life resulting in both emotional and physical symptoms such as feeling out of control, feeling weepy, sleeping problems, panic attacks, headaches, muscle tension and dizziness.

Anxiety and fear are basic survival instincts, when you become anxious, your mind and body is telling you that you are in a dangerous situation and your body will release hormones such as Adrenaline, preparing you for the fight or flight response. Your mind can't tell the difference between imagination and reality and so extreme worrying and negative thinking can trigger the same response.

Through various techniques and relaxation, Solution Focused Hypnotherapy will help you to replace those negative thinking patterns with more beneficial thought processes, reducing anxiety levels and unlocking positive change so that you can look forward to a happier future, free from worry and anxiety.
